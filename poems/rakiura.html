Rakiura Track

32 km · Stewart Island/Rakiura

Moss blankets spongey dirt,
In dappled light ferns flirt.
Ancient rimu drapes from high,
Birdsong fills the cloudless sky.

Stillness I've not met elsewhere,
Rakiura, beyond compare.
Happy hour just in time,
Beer by the beach, the finish line.
